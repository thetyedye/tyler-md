- A Nextra (Next.js) site published to `tyler.md`.
- One MDX file per top-level route in `pages/`.

## Voice and quality rules

- First person, declarative. No "I'm passionate about" or "leveraging cutting-edge AI."
- Specific over impressive. Concrete numbers, real workflows, named tools — not adjectives.
- Short. Every page should read in under 90 seconds.
- Tyler's vocabulary: *agentic OS, agency brain, operational graph, workflow skills, source-backed output, combat the slop, quality or slop.*
- No emojis. The site doesn't use them.
- The Lab is **being built**, not shipped. Use "building" / "shipping" / "first workflow live" honestly.

The site's north star: *create simple, useful products with care, taste, and attention. Slow down where it matters. Combat the slop.*
