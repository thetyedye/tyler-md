# The Lab

*Strategy Labs' product studio.*

The Lab is the umbrella Strategy Labs uses to take new tools to market fast, in proof-of-concept form, without the weight of a full brand launch. Closest reference is [Every](https://every.to) — an AI consulting firm that builds internal tools, betas them under an umbrella, and only spins them out as standalone products once they've proven traction.

The model is lean-startup: alpha-test with friendlies and existing clients, iterate on real feedback, kill ideas that don't get traction, invest in branding and standalone GTM only when a tool earns its way out.

## Current working-name products

- **Signal** — call analysis dashboard. Sentiment, commitments, time tracking, surfacing missed opportunities.
- **Benchmarking** — cross-brand performance comparison, built for brands.
- **Contirbution Margin Forecaster* — profitability forecaster that merges business profit with marketing kpi's.
- More to come — The Lab is the home for whatever's next.
