# About

I grew up in Washington State, around water and trees. Still need to be outside and moving.

I wasn't the kid taking apart electronics. I loved tech and music, but what drew me in was softer: I like figuring out how things connect, understanding something complex, and explaining it without losing people. Marketing stuck because it's creative problem-solving, and it's adaptive — the industry shifts, I shift with it.

## What I care about

Quality starts with user experience. I want to build things I'd actually use. When a project doesn't feel right, my motivation drops out — I've learned to trust that signal.

## What I'm chasing

Agents as the operating layer underneath service companies. What I'm building with The Lab is the beginning: predictable workflows, tools that change how teams work, and a system that sits underneath the company instead of on top of it. Transform how an agency operates without losing the human part.

## Outside work

Guitar, piano, running (training for a marathon), golf, photography. I have a mini Australian shepherd who runs the household.

If I'm not building something, I'm outside.
