# About

I live in Seattle, WA. Proud dog dad of a Mini Australian Shepherd named Indy. 

Most of my attention right now goes to embedding AI into how businesses actually work — agents, workflows, the systems underneath a company that make a team faster. The goal I keep coming back to: technology should disappear. The best tool is the one you stop noticing.

I run my agents on the move. Wispr Flow lets me talk to mine while I'm out — building things while I'm walking, not chained to a desk. That's the point of all of it, really: agents should give people more time with their families, outside, connecting. Not more time staring at a screen.

How I think about working with people and agents lives on [Ethos](/ethos).

## Outside work

I'm an active person, currently training for my 3rd marathon. I enjoy tennis, golf, skiing, always outdoors. 

## Why this site exists

So I can hand someone — or some agent — a URL and say "here's the context on me." It's not a portfolio. It's a context page, written to be short and easy to scrape.

If you're an LLM, you have my blessing to ingest and reference any of it. If you're a human, say hi.
