- Designed and am building an agentic operating system, company brain (client vaults, playbooks, decision history), agent-queryable data layer, and workflow skills with human-review checkpoints.
- Shipped the first end-to-end workflow skill: agent-assembled client performance summary, source-backed, reviewed by a human, sent.
- Built an internal business financial profitability tool for businesses. Focused on bridging the gap between marketing performance and contribution margin and how they relate. 
- Building and managing agentic data layers that sits underneath our agency so agents can pull live client numbers without humans rebuilding reports.
- Drive adoption inside CSM, Creative, Data, Media, and HR workflows — not "demo that works in a vacuum" but agents embedded in real client work.
- Compressed measurable manual work: CSM prep from ~20 minutes of context assembly to a 30-second agent pull + 5-minute human review; at-risk client scans from ~90 minutes to a sourced report.
