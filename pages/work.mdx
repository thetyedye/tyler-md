# Work

## Strategy Labs · Marketing Data Specialist → Agentic Systems
*2021 – Present*

I run data at Strategy Labs, a boutique digital marketing agency. Started in marketing-data plumbing — Funnel.io pipelines, Supabase warehouses, reporting infrastructure. Now most of my work is building AI tools and systems that help turn the agency's data and context into something agents can reliably operate on.

- Designed and am building an agentic operating system underneath the agency: company brain (client vaults, playbooks, decision history), agent-queryable data layer that pulls live client numbers without humans rebuilding reports, and workflow skills with human-review checkpoints.
- Shipped the first end-to-end workflow skill: agent-assembled client performance summary, source-backed, reviewed by a human, sent.
- Built a contribution-margin app: a standalone calculator that takes business-level profitability metrics and forecasts how marketing inputs (revenue, ad spend, CPC, CTR, AOV, CPM) back into profit. Gives the business clear profit tracking and gives the marketing team KPI goals that actually align with margin.
- Drive adoption inside CSM, Creative, Data, Media, and HR workflows — not "demo that works in a vacuum" but agents embedded in real client work.
- Drive AI adoption across the team: building skills people reach for, educating, and communicating what's possible — the human-to-human side of going AI-native, not just shipping tools.

### What the work covers

- **Data platform architecture** — schema design, ingestion pipelines (Funnel.io), Supabase warehouses, and the query and reporting layers on top.
- **Applied AI product development** — shipping AI-native products with LLMs (primarily Claude), agent frameworks, and workflow skills. Prototypes, but also production systems with real users.
- **Internal AI adoption** — training teams, embedding agents into workflows, building internal tooling that makes AI-generated data usable by people who've never written a prompt.
- **Business-to-data translation** — working with Ops and leadership to surface the right questions, then building the infrastructure to answer them.
- **Automation and integration** — pipelines connecting data across ad platforms, CRMs, e-commerce, and internal tools.
- **Marketing operations and growth** — paid media, CRO, and attribution work. The data infrastructure work grew out of those marketing problems.
